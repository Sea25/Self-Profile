Pass With Ease

Our project, Pass With Ease, is a student-friendly web platform designed to simplify buying, selling, and exchanging books while promoting learning.

It features dynamic book listings with search functionality, allowing users to upload books for exchange or sale, including editable prices for sellers. The tutoring section offers educational videos in a responsive grid layout, and a contact section includes a form and an embedded Google Map for location details.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the platform boasts a clean, modern design with interactive elements like hover effects and modals. Future enhancements could include backend integration, user authentication, and payment gateways, making it a one-stop solution for affordable learning resources and book sharing.

VeriSafe

Our project, VeriSafe, is an all-in-one solution for campus safety. Featuring emergency alerts, live location sharing, emergency contacts, and spam detection. Stay safe, stay secure.

SafeCampus simplifies safety measures by integrating these features into one platform, making it easier for users to stay secure and respond effectively to emergencies. It promotes peace of mind and a safer campus environment for everyone.

Emergency Alerts: Instantly notify campus security during emergencies, ensuring quick response times.

Live Location Sharing: Share real-time location with trusted contacts, enabling faster assistance in critical situations.

Emergency Contacts: Provide easy access to important emergency numbers and services, saving time during crises.

Spam Checker: Detect and avoid suspicious links or files, protecting users from online threats.
