About Me

I'm a B.Tech IT student at CUSAT, passionate about technology, innovation, and community building. I've participated in hackathons and projects like a voice changer and love working on creative tech solutions.

Why Women in Tech?

I believe in making tech more accessible for women and day scholars. My goal is to introduce flexible event formats, hybrid workshops, and mentorship programs to ensure equal opportunities for all.

What Change Can I Bring?

  • Flexible Events: Organizing events at better times or online to include more women.
  • Mentorship & Community: Creating a space where seniors guide juniors.
  • Spreading Awareness: Sharing opportunities like hackathons, workshops, and internships.
  • Skill-Based Learning: Hosting hands-on coding and project sessions for confidence building.
